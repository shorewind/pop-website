Google Calendar

You can subscribe to our Google Calendar so you'll automatically see what meetings and events we've got planned! Just follow these steps to subscribe to the calendar:

  • On Google Calendar, look for the "Other Calendars" section in the bottom of the left menu.
  • Click the plus button, then select the "Subscribe to Calendar" option.
  • In the text box, enter "acm@uah.edu" to search for our ACM calendar.
  • Once you've added our calendar, you can rename it or change the calendar color!
